    • Newer Wi-Fi + Cellular models include built-in eSIM technology that acts as a digital SIM card. There are a few ways to activate an eSIM on your iPad, including downloading it to the iPad itself, scanning a QR code, or using an app from a network provider.* With eSIM, you can choose mobile data plans from selected network providers in the UK and in more than 180 countries and regions around the world. Learn more about setting up eSIM.

      Wi-Fi + Cellular models with embedded eSIM technology include iPad Pro 12.9-inch (3rd generation or later), iPad Pro 11-inch (1st generation or later), iPad Air (3rd generation or later), iPad (7th generation or later), and iPad mini (5th and 6th generation).
